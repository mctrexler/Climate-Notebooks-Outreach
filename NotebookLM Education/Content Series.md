# NotebookLM Education — Content Series

A planned sequence of education content. These should ship before or alongside the subscription pitch — awareness must precede conversion.

---

## LinkedIn Series: "What NotebookLM Actually Does"

Suggested sequence for Monday education posts. Each is a standalone post, but they build on each other.

1. **"This is not a chatbot."** What makes NotebookLM different from ChatGPT and Google — it only draws from the documents you give it. No hallucination. No open web.
2. **"We gave it 100,000 sources."** Introducing The Climate Web corpus. What it is, how long it took to build, why it matters.
3. **"I asked it a question no report has ever answered."** A specific example — the question, the answer, the citations. Show the product working.
4. **"What it feels like to have an analyst who has read everything."** The experience of using it: ask, refine, follow threads. A question that takes a researcher weeks, answered in minutes.
5. **"Why this matters for climate specifically."** The climate information problem — too much, too fragmented, too technical. Why NotebookLM solves a real problem here.
6. **"Who built this, and why."** The Climatographers story. 35 years. Why we built The Climate Web. Why we put it in NotebookLM.

## Webinar Series

### Webinar 1 — General Audience
**"What is NotebookLM and why does it change how you engage with climate intelligence?"**
- Audience: Individuals / general interest
- Format: 45 min — 20 min demo, 15 min Q&A, 10 min on notebooks and subscription
- Key demo question: Something a curious non-specialist would ask (e.g., "What are the most underappreciated climate risks?")
- CTA: General interest subscription

### Webinar 2 — Business/Tracking
**"Climate risk for business: how NotebookLM turns 100,000 sources into an on-demand analyst"**
- Audience: Sustainability officers, ESG leads, risk managers
- Format: 45 min — 20 min demo, 15 min Q&A, 10 min on business subscription
- Key demo question: A real business risk question (e.g., "What are the regulatory developments most likely to affect US manufacturers in the next five years?")
- CTA: Business subscription

### Webinar 3 — Business/Advisory
**"Beyond the report: building a custom climate knowledge system for your organization"**
- Audience: Organizations considering advisory services
- Format: 45 min — 20 min demo/walkthrough, 15 min Q&A, 10 min on advisory services
- Key demo question: Show a custom-configured notebook for a specific sector
- CTA: Advisory services inquiry

## Email Sequence

A 3-email onboarding sequence for new list subscribers before the subscription pitch:

1. **Email 1:** "You're on the list — here's what we're building." Brief intro to The Climatographers and the notebooks. One link to a demo clip or LinkedIn post.
2. **Email 2:** "What NotebookLM is and why it's different." The core education message. One concrete example.
3. **Email 3:** "Here's what you get access to." Introduce the subscription options. CTA to subscribe or book a call.
