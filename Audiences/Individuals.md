# Audience: Concerned Individuals

**Product:** General interest subscription — climatenotebooks.net

---

## Who They Are

Climate-engaged general public: people who follow climate news, worry about climate change, want to understand it more deeply, and are frustrated by superficial coverage. They may be professionals in unrelated fields, retirees, educators, students, or activists. They are not climate scientists but they are not uninformed.

They have probably never heard of NotebookLM.

---

## What They Want

- To understand climate change better than the news gives them
- Access to the kind of depth and synthesis that usually requires professional immersion
- To feel like they are engaging with the real picture, not a simplified version of it
- Trustworthy, authoritative sources — not advocacy or spin

---

## The Core Pitch

*Nearly 40 years of professional climate immersion, distilled into notebooks you can actually use. Ask any climate question and get an answer drawn from 100,000+ vetted sources — not a Google search, not a chatbot hallucinating.*

---

## Messaging Notes

- Lead with the depth and credibility of the underlying corpus
- NotebookLM education is essential here — this audience has never seen anything like it
- Avoid jargon; this audience is smart but not specialist
- The frustration with superficial climate coverage is a live nerve — name it
- Price point and accessibility matter — position as affordable for engaged individuals

---

## Channel Priorities

1. LinkedIn (Monday education posts, Friday engagement posts)
2. Facebook
3. Email newsletter
4. Webinar: general audience session

---

## Content That Works for This Audience

- "What the notebooks can tell you that no single report can"
- Specific, surprising findings surfaced from the corpus
- NotebookLM demos using questions a curious non-specialist would actually ask
- The story of The Climatographers — 40 years, what we've seen, why this matters
